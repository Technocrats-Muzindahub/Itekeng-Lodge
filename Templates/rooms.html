About Our Rooms

We have eight luxury suites split over 2 camps. Two are family suites and six are double suites. The double suites sleep two people (double or twin), and family suites sleep two people (double or twin) downstairs, and two people (twin) in the open loft. All the suites are spacious and fully air-conditioned.

Each suite has an ample en-suite bathroom with modern finishes (with bath and shower), comfy seating area and private patio. There is a stocked mini bar in each room, which will be charged for on departure. There is also a complimentary tea and coffee station in each suite.

The main entertainment area is open plan, and has a lounge with a TV, DSTV and DVD player, a beautiful bar area with pool table and various board games, and an outside patio area that seats up to ten people
