Our Executive Rooms

Prime executive rooms are comfortable with a clean, modern and cultural design. All single rooms come with an en-suite bathroom with walk-in shower and free toiletries, an LCD Smart TV, tea and coffee making facilities and a desk.

Comfort and Safety are our priority at Itekeng Lodge, and this is why each suite has a safe box where customers can store their important documents during their stay in the hotel.

Maximum occupancy is 2 guests, but an extra bed may be available on request at an extra charge
